About

Google Developer Groups on Campus – DSU (GDGOC-DSU) is a student-led, non-profit tech community powered by Google Developers. Our mission is to empower students to learn, build, and innovate using modern technologies from the Google ecosystem and beyond.

At GDGOC-DSU, we provide an inclusive platform for students from Computer Science, Software Engineering, Electrical Engineering, and related disciplines to explore real-world problem-solving through technologies like Android, Web, Flutter, Firebase, TensorFlow, and Google Cloud.

The community is currently lead by Saad Jangda and co-lead by Hani Zehra, fostering a culture of collaboration, innovation, and growth helping students become the next generation of developers, leaders, and changemakers.
